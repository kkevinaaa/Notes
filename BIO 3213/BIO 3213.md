## Amino Acids

Each amino acid contains:
- an amino group
- a carboxyl group
- a variable side chain / R group


# Protein Structure

**Primary:** amino acid sequence

**Secondary structure:** alpha helix, beta sheet and loops
- Formed and stabilized by hydrogen bonding, electrostatic and van der Waals interactions.
**Tertiary:** Phi-Psi angle
- Protein packing yields tertiary structure.
**Quaternary:** arrangement of several polypeptide chains
- Involves interaction among tertiary structure elements of separate polymer chains.
